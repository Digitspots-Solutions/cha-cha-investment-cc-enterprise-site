About Us

Cha Cha Investment, CC Enterprise is Osogbo's trusted name in fashion and creative design. Located at Adeen former filling station powerline, we've built our reputation on delivering exceptional quality, innovative designs, and personalized service. Whether you're looking for traditional attire, contemporary fashion, or custom creative designs, our skilled team brings years of experience and passion to every project. We understand the importance of looking your best for every occasion, and we're committed to making that happen with style, precision, and creativity.
